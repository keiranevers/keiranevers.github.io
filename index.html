About Me


Keira is currently employed with Liberty Mutual Insurance Group as a Systems Analyst for the Personal Insurance IT group. But, only until February 8, 2018. She started at Liberty Mutual on February 20, 2012. Which, happens to be the same day as her mother's birthday, as well as the same day her Grandmother died in 2016.

Keira has accepted an offer from Measured Progress as a Senior Business Analyst! She will begin working for Measured Progress on February 20, 2018. Wow! That's the same anniversary date she had with Liberty Mutual!

Keira is married to her husband David and lives in East Wakefield, New Hampshire where they share their home with three rescue cats and a rescue Great Pyrenees, Ruby.
